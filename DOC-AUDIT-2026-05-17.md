## Doctrine note

This audit produced **net positive correctness** but the long-tail risk in 230 markdown files is structural — content drift accumulates faster than humans can chase it. Three durable mitigations exist:

1. **`scripts/check-docs-commands-drift.sh`** — already catches per-flag drift between binary `--help` and `docs/commands/`. Drift-checker green at 14/0/0/0.
2. **`scripts/check-canonical-db-shape.sh`** (#723, this round) — catches DB-state drift before triage.
3. **The docs-mirror rule** (`feedback_docs_mirror_cli` memory) — when you change a CLI surface, update `docs/commands/<cmd>/` in the same PR. Self-enforces structural drift.

A fourth mitigation worth filing as a v1.3.x ticket: a content-drift detector that scans for stale schema version numbers + stale entry counts + broken cross-file links across the full `docs/` tree. Could be a 30-line shell script. Out of scope for v1.2.x.
